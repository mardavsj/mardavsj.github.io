Spotlight picks with quick-start tips

We rotate these every week based on player feedback and stability checks.

Sudoku Classic

Start on the 4x4 and 6x6 boards to learn patterns. Toggle notes to mark candidates and avoid restarts.

Chess

Perfect for 10-minute breaks. Use the undo-friendly casual mode to practice openings and recover from blunders.

Bubble Shooter

Plan off-the-wall bounces, clear the middle first, and use color swaps to avoid dead shots and rushed reloads.

Maze Roll

Mobile-friendly tilt controls; reduce sensitivity in settings if the ball overshoots corners.
